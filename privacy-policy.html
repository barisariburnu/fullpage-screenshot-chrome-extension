Full Page Screenshot - Privacy Policy

1. Data Collection

Full Page Screenshot extension prioritizes user privacy and data protection. Our extension:

  • Does not collect any personal information
  • Does not store any usage data
  • Does not utilize third-party services
  • Does not implement analytics or tracking tools

2. Permissions and Usage Purpose

Our extension requires the following permissions for essential functionality:

  • activeTab: Required to capture screenshots of the active browser tab
  • downloads: Required to save captured screenshots to your local device
  • debugger: Required for full-page screenshot functionality
  • <all_urls>: Required to operate across all websites

3. Data Security

Our extension maintains strict security standards:

  • Screenshots are stored locally only
  • No data transmission over the internet
  • Screenshots are saved directly to the user's device
